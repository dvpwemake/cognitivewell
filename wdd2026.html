WHY THIS MATTERS

Dementia can strike anyone.
Early screening changes everything.

Up to 51% of Mild Cognitive Impairment cases can be halted or reversed — but only if caught in time. 92% of Americans are never diagnosed until it is too late.

Bruce Willis • Frontotemporal Dementia (2023)
His family raised global awareness of FTD.
Ronald Reagan • Alzheimer's (1994)
His public letter remains one of the most powerful calls for awareness.
