Congratulations! You just completed the AD8-based cognitive condition self-assessment.

This tool is designed for the early detection of mild cognitive impairment (MCI) and dementia. A positive screen (score ≥2) indicates the need for further comprehensive evaluation, such as full neuropsychological testing and clinical assessment. The accuracy of the assessment depends on a reliable informant (the self-report version is less ideal in advanced stages). It is free for clinical use, with permissions granted for non-commercial purposes.

Important: This self-assessment is a screening tool only and does not constitute a medical diagnosis. Please share your results with a qualified healthcare provider for clinical evaluation. If you are experiencing urgent concerns, contact your physician or emergency services.
